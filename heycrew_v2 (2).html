How it works

Three steps to a plan everyone loves

STEP 01

Create your event

Name it, pick an emoji, set a few candidate dates. Takes about 30 seconds — no account, no email.

STEP 02

Invite your group

Share a code or a link. Everyone joins instantly on their own phone — no sign-up for anyone.

STEP 03

Plan together

Everyone marks availability and votes on ideas. HeyCrew surfaces the best dates and top picks automatically.

Features

Everything your group needs

Smart availability calendar

Every member marks when they're free. HeyCrew finds the dates with the most overlap and ranks them — no spreadsheets, no back-and-forth, no guessing.

💡

Collaborative ideas board

Suggest hotels, restaurants and activities. The group votes and the best ideas rise to the top, organised by Where, Stay, Eat and Do.

🗺️

Itinerary builder

Turn your group's best ideas into a day-by-day timeline. Assign tasks, track to-dos, and see conflicts flagged automatically.

🗳️

Group polls

Can't decide? Put it to a vote. Poll anything — destination, restaurant, hotel — with results that update live.

Plus
💬

Event chat

A dedicated chat per group with @mentions, link previews and real-time messages — kept separate from your main chats.

📤

Export & share

Export your plan as a PDF or CSV, sync confirmed dates to your calendar, and share your itinerary in one tap.
