How it works

Three steps to
a plan everyone loves

1

Create your event

Pick an emoji, name your event, set some dates. Takes 30 seconds. No account, no email, no hassle.

2

Invite your group

Share a code or link. Your group joins instantly on their own phone — no sign-ups required for anyone.

3

Plan together

Everyone marks availability, votes on ideas, and the app surfaces the best dates and top picks automatically.

Features

Everything your group
needs. Nothing it doesn't.

Smart Availability Calendar

Every member marks when they're free. HeyCrew automatically finds the dates with the most overlap and ranks them — no spreadsheets, no back-and-forth, no guessing.

💡

Collaborative Ideas Board

Suggest hotels, restaurants, activities and more. The group votes, the best ideas rise to the top. Organised by category — Where, Stay, Eat, Do and more.

🗺️

Itinerary Builder

Turn your group's best ideas into a day-by-day timeline. Assign tasks, track to-dos, and see conflicts flagged automatically.

🗳️

Group Polls

Can't decide? Put it to a vote. Create polls on anything — best destination, which restaurant, which hotel. Results update live.

⭐ Plus
💬

Event Chat

A dedicated chat for your group. @mentions, link previews, real-time messages — separate from your main chats so nothing gets lost.

📤

Export & Share

Export your plan as a PDF or CSV. Sync confirmed dates to your calendar. Share your itinerary with anyone in one tap.
