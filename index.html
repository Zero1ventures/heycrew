How it works

Three steps to a plan everyone's actually in on

01

Start the event

Name it, pick an emoji, throw out a few dates. Thirty seconds, no account, no email.

02

Get the crew in

Send a code or a link. Everyone joins on their own phone, no sign-up required.

03

Let HeyCrew do the work

People mark when they're free and vote on ideas. HeyCrew shows what's actually winning.

Features

Everything the group actually needs

📅

Smart availability calendar

Everyone marks when they're free — HeyCrew finds the dates with the most overlap and ranks them. No spreadsheets, no guessing.

💡

Ideas board

Drop in hotels, restaurants, activities. The group votes and the best ones rise, sorted by Where, Stay, Eat, Do.

🗺️

Itinerary builder

Turn the winning ideas into a day-by-day plan. Assign tasks and see conflicts flagged automatically.

🗳️

Group polls

Can't decide? Put it to a vote — results update live for everyone.

💬

Event chat

One dedicated thread per event, kept separate from your regular group chats.

📤

Export & share

Export as PDF or CSV, sync confirmed dates to your calendar, share the itinerary in one tap.
